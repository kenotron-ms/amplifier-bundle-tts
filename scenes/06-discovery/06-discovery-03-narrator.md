# AUDIO PROFILE: Narrator — “The Wall”
## “In a deep pool of blood lay his daughter.”

## THE SCENE: The bathroom of the room, lower level of the megabuilding — the moment everything changes
A dirty tiled wall covered in grime. Then not. Beyond the wall: a small damp chamber, barely room enough for what is inside. The cannon on Blaze’s arm hums and glows. The air lights up. The wall is gone.

### DIRECTOR’S NOTES
Style:
* Grave and devastatingly even — the narrator does not flinch; the flatness of “In a deep pool of blood lay his daughter” is the entire register
* The body’s description is clinical, which makes it unbearable; resist any impulse to soften, dramatize, or editorialize
Pacing: Slow approach to the wall; the cannon blast is a single sharp break; then the horror arrives in complete stillness
Accent: Neutral, grave — this is the most important passage in the production

### SAMPLE CONTEXT
Blaze’s men claimed they searched the room. He found the hollow wall they missed. He does what no one else would have had the authority to do: he blasts it open. What is behind the wall is the reason for everything that follows — including the ending. This passage must be delivered with complete emotional control.

#### TRANSCRIPT
“And?”

“Nothing.”

“Then you weren’t looking hard enough,” Blaze said, walking over to the leftmost tiled wall. It was covered in dirt and grime, surely it couldn’t be moved. He pressed his hand up to it, feeling for a mechanism. Nothing. He knocked on the wall to feel if it was hollow. Sure enough, it was. “Told you.”

His men grunted as he took a step back, holding his arm towards the wall. In one smooth motion, the top of his arm folded out into a small cannon, the cyberware humming as it obeyed his mental command. He motioned for his men to take a step back as the cannon started to glow. With a loud boom, the air lit up as it blasted open the wall, nearly completely obliterating it, revealing a small damp chamber behind it.

Blaze lowered his arm, the cannon folding back into his forearm. His eyes widened as he looked inside the chamber. In a deep pool of blood lay his daughter. Her limbs were missing as well as her clothes. Her face was frozen in terror. Blaze clenched his teeth as the white hot fuel of rage filled his veins. His men took a step back in a moment of incredible intelligence as Blaze yelled at the top of his lungs, reaching down to the corpse of his daughter.
