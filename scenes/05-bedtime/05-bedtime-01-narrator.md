# AUDIO PROFILE: Narrator — “Beside the Boy”
## “Lia passed him, heading to bed, trusting Chase with Jack.”

## THE SCENE: Jack’s bedroom, Chroma apartment, late night
A small child’s room at the back of the apartment. A narrow bed. A nightlight. The city is muffled here—just low ambient glow filtering through the curtains and the faint hum of the building. Chase sits on the edge of the bed in his work clothes, still carrying the weight of the whole night.

### DIRECTOR’S NOTES
Style:
* Tender and slightly aching — the narrator carries the knowledge of what the stuffed animal cost and where it came from
* The warmth is genuine; so is the shadow underneath it
Pacing: Slow and soft; lullaby register; a single sentence that opens the scene
Accent: Neutral, gentle

### SAMPLE CONTEXT
Lia has gone to bed, leaving Chase alone with Jack. The kitchen argument is behind them. The credits are on the counter. Chase sits on the edge of Jack’s bed with a pink stuffed dog missing one ear and something he needs to put somewhere. He will put it into a story.

#### TRANSCRIPT
Chase said, moving to lead Jack to his bed. Lia passed him, heading to bed, trusting Chase with Jack.
