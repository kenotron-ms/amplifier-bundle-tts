# AUDIO PROFILE: Chase + Jack — “The Story About the Daemon”
## “But the worst thing he did was have a daughter.”

## THE SCENE: Jack’s bedroom, Chroma apartment, late night
A small child’s room at the back of the apartment. A narrow bed. A nightlight. The city is muffled here—just low ambient glow filtering through the curtains and the faint hum of the building. Chase sits on the edge of the bed in his work clothes, still carrying the weight of the whole night.

### DIRECTOR’S NOTES
Style:
* Chase (Algenib, Gravelly): gentle and deliberate in story-telling mode; he is confessing in the language of bedtime; his voice softens when Jack interrupts him; lands quietly on “she gave him a pink stuffed animal dog, even though it was missing an ear”
* Jack (Leda, Youthful): fully awake with story-energy; “Yayy!” is completely unselfconscious; “That’s like my one” is [under his breath] near-inaudible wonder
Pacing: Story register — rhythmic rises and character responses; Chase pauses to choose words carefully
Accent: Chroma mid-city, relaxed register

### SAMPLE CONTEXT
Chase is telling Jack the true story encoded as myth. The bad man is Blaze. The daughter is the girl Chase rescued. The hero—Angel, Daemon—is Chase. Jack doesn’t know yet that his father is the Daemon. He doesn’t know yet that the stuffed animal now in his hands belonged to a dead girl. Chase doesn’t know yet that the story will be heard by the man it’s about.

#### TRANSCRIPT
“This story is about a very bad man,” Chase said, sitting on the bed beside Jack. “He stole and robbed and killed people… But the worst thing he did was have a daughter.”

“How is that a bad thing, dad?” Jack said, looking up at Chase.

“Because he didn’t love her, and tried to sell her to very bad people.”

“That’s so mean!” Jack interrupted, “He’s not a good dad like you!”

Chase chuckled, “Yeah he wasn’t.” He paused, “But that’s not where the story ends, because this story has a hero.”

“Angel!” Jack shouted.

“Yes, Angel. He found the girl and her dad wasn’t even there with her! She was with her dad’s coworkers. Daemon knew the girl was in danger and saved her!”

“Yayy!” Jack said enthusiastically.

“He brought her to a new house with a new family and in return… she gave him a pink stuffed animal dog, even though it was missing an ear.”

“That’s like my one.” Jack said under his breath.

“The end!” Chase said, “Time to go to bed. I’ll be here all day tomorrow, so I can tell you stories the whole day.”
