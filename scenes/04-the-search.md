# AUDIO PROFILE: Narrator — "South Atlantic"
## The Search Scene

## THE SCENE: South Atlantic District, Three Megabuildings, Night
The South Atlantic district belongs to the Scavs — or it did, until Blaze
walked in. His crew moves in three columns through three towers,
room by room, floor by floor. The Scavs who try anything don't try twice.
Blaze's group of six men search the first megabuilding with the focused
efficiency of people who have done this before.

### DIRECTOR'S NOTES
Style: Procedural and controlled. This scene is about method — Blaze as a
hunter, not a monster. Cold competence. The tension lives in what they might find.
Pacing: Steady. The phone call in the middle is the pivot point — slow down
slightly there.
Accent: Neutral American. Crisp consonants. No emotion wasted.

### SAMPLE CONTEXT
Blaze has a lead: South Atlantic, one of three megabuildings.
His daughter is missing — taken by someone called the Daemon.
He is getting closer. He does not yet know what he will find.

#### TRANSCRIPT
[informative, measured] Blaze split his crew into three groups —
twenty-six men across three towers, each working from the top down.
The Scavs owned South Atlantic. Past tense, now.

Blaze's group reached the last room on the last floor of the first building.
He stood in the doorway and scanned it. Plain. Empty. Nothing.

[a phone call — soft chime]

He answered with a nod.

Yes? Find anything?

[a voice on the line] We did, sir. A fragment of a datachip.
And footage from a nearby security camera.

Good. I'm heading your way. Upload the footage — I'll review it en route.

[pause]

There's something else, sir. A small... blood-soaked cloth.

[longer pause]

[quietly, controlled] Blaze absorbed this. The rage was there —
it was always there — but he could use rage or he could spend it.
He chose to use it.

Good find, he said through his teeth. Any DNA?

Yes, sir. It's a match.

[measured, final] He ended the call without another word
and walked toward the exit, the city's neon bleeding through the windows above him.
