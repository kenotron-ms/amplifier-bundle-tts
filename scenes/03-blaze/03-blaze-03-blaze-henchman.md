# AUDIO PROFILE: Blaze + Henchman — “The Lead”
## “Then let’s get going. We have a Daemon to catch.”

## THE SCENE: A concrete warehouse in lower Chroma, interrogation room — immediately after the shot
The gun smoke is still in the air. Crum’s blood spreads across the concrete. Blaze addresses the remaining men, handgun still warm. His men stand very still. One slender younger man has prepared an answer.

### DIRECTOR’S NOTES
Style:
* Blaze (Alnilam, Firm): composed, practically administrative — the killing is already behind him; “Where.” is a single word with a period, not a question
* Henchman (Charon, Informative): careful and precise; he has prepared his information because he knows his life depends on having something useful; delivery is measured and respectful
Pacing: Business-like; efficiency is survival in this room; every line is short and purposeful
Accent: Blaze — tight authority; Henchman — formal, clipped

### SAMPLE CONTEXT
One man is dead. The remaining men must demonstrate they are useful. The slender younger man steps forward with a lead: South Atlantic District, three megabuildings. It is enough. Blaze holsters the gun and moves. The search for the Daemon begins.

#### TRANSCRIPT
“Any of you got a good explanation for your incompetence?” Blaze said, waving his handgun across the room.

A slender younger man stepped forward and bowed. “We never meant to lose her, but we have a lead on her whereabouts.”

Blaze turned towards him. “Where.”

“South Atlantic, we suspect their base is in one of the megabuildings there, which narrows it down to three.”

Blaze stepped down from the raised section of the floor, holstering his gun. “Then let’s get going. We have a Daemon to catch.”
