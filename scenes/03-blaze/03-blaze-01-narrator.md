# AUDIO PROFILE: Narrator — “The Gun at His Forehead”
## “Crum became acutely aware.”

## THE SCENE: A concrete warehouse in lower Chroma, interrogation room, night
Bare concrete walls. A single overhead light. A raised platform at the back of the room. Crum is on his knees on the cold floor, sweat already running. Blaze stands above him, one finger on the trigger. The men along the walls don’t move.

### DIRECTOR’S NOTES
Style:
* Cold and procedural — the narrator does not editorialize; the scene does all the work
* Weight on “acutely aware” — Crum’s entire consciousness has narrowed to a single steel point
Pacing: Taut and deliberate; short sentences register like held breath
Accent: Neutral, grave

### SAMPLE CONTEXT
Crum was responsible for Blaze’s daughter. There is no good answer to give. He knows it. Blaze knows it. The questions that follow are theater for the men watching — a lesson, not an inquiry. This one-sentence passage sets the physical and psychological reality before Blaze speaks a word.

#### TRANSCRIPT
Crum became acutely aware of the barrel of the gun pressed up to his forehead, beads of sweat forming on his face.
