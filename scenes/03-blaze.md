    # AUDIO PROFILE: Narrator — "The Father of Blood"
    ## The Blaze Scene

    ## THE SCENE: Blaze's Territory, Somewhere in Helios
    Concrete floors. Leather gloves. A man on his knees. This is a room where
    people beg and it doesn't help. Blaze stands above Crum with a gun barrel
    pressed to the man's forehead, and the only thing moving is a bead of sweat.
    The men along the walls know better than to breathe too loud.

    ### DIRECTOR'S NOTES
    Style: Controlled menace. The narrator is unhurried — almost clinical — the
    way violence becomes ordinary when it happens often enough. Blaze speaks in
    complete sentences. He is not a man who shouts. His calm is what makes him
    terrifying.
    Pacing: Deliberate. Blaze's questions land like weights being placed on a scale.
    Accent: American, precise. No warmth anywhere in the room.

    ### SAMPLE CONTEXT
    Blaze's daughter is missing. He tasked Crum with finding her.
    Crum failed. This is the consequence.
    The daughter is central to everything — her absence is the engine of the story.

    #### TRANSCRIPT
    [gravelly, measured] Blaze became acutely aware of the barrel of the gun
    pressed against Crum's forehead — if you could call the quivering wreck
    before him a man anymore.

    You gonna tell me again how it is you lost my daughter?
    he said — one finger resting lightly on the trigger,
    leather gloves creaking in the silence.

    [panicked, voice wavering] I-it was the Daemon, sir. We didn't mean to —

    [coldly] I seem to remember you saying you could handle him.
    [pause]
    I remember that very clearly.

    [shouting] Please — no! We didn't mean to!

    [a gunshot — flat, final — then silence]

    [gravelly] Blaze lowered the gun and turned to the others.

    Any of you got a good explanation for your incompetence?

    [pause]

    A younger man stepped forward and bowed.

    We never meant to lose her, sir. But we have a lead on her whereabouts.
    South Atlantic. Their base is in one of the megabuildings there —
    which narrows it down to three.

    [slowly, deliberate] Blaze holstered his weapon and stepped down from the raised floor.

    Then let's get going.

    [pause — the sound of footsteps, the room exhaling]

    We have a Daemon to catch.
