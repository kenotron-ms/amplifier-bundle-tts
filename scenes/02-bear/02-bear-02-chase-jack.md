# AUDIO PROFILE: Chase + Jack — “The Pink Dog”
## “I found this for you at work!”

## THE SCENE: Chroma District apartment, hallway and Jack’s bedroom, late night
The apartment hallway leading to the back bedrooms. The neon glow from the living room window fades as they move deeper in. Jack’s room is dark except for the faint bleed of city light under the door. The apartment is beginning to quiet for the night.

### DIRECTOR’S NOTES
Style:
* Chase (Algenib, Gravelly): tender and careful; his exhaustion is real but he is fully present for Jack; the evasion about where the gift came from is practiced, gentle
* Jack (Leda, Youthful): half-asleep wonder; complete trust; notices the blood stains on Chase’s jacket and ignores them; the [drowsy] “…Yes” is barely above a whisper
Pacing: Slow and warm; unhurried bedtime rhythm; Chase is buying time with kindness
Accent: Chroma mid-city, relaxed register

### SAMPLE CONTEXT
Chase deflects the heavy conversation by producing a gift: a small pink stuffed animal missing one ear, taken from a job he won’t describe. Jack doesn’t ask where it came from. He notices his dad’s bloodstained jacket and says nothing. Chase leads him to bed and offers a story. The stuffed animal will appear again at the end of everything.

#### TRANSCRIPT
“Aw I’m sorry you lost your bear, but I have a present for you!”

Chase rummaged through his bag until he produced a small pink stuffed animal missing its ear. He handed it to Jack and patted him on the head.

“I found this for you at work!” Chase said, moving to lead Jack to his bed. Lia passed him, heading to bed, trusting Chase with Jack.

“Really?”

“Yeah, now you said you couldn’t sleep?”

Jack looked up at Chase, ignoring the light blood stains on his jacket. [drowsy] “…Yes.”

“Do you want me to tell you a story?”
