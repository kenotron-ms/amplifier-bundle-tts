# AUDIO PROFILE: Blaze + Henchman — “The Cloth”
## “Yes sir, it’s a match.”

## THE SCENE: South Atlantic megabuilding, first-floor corridor — Blaze turning to leave an empty room
The bare apartment is behind him. Blaze is already in motion as he answers the call. An empty hallway. Somewhere across the district, his man stands over a bloodstained cloth on a concrete floor.

### DIRECTOR’S NOTES
Style:
* Blaze (Alnilam, Firm): controlled even when the cloth news arrives; the suppressed rage surfaces only in the gritted “Good find” — deliver that line [through gritted teeth] per the story
* Henchman (Charon, Informative): hesitates before “A small bloodsoaked cloth” — he knows what this information will cost the room; careful delivery, not quite monotone
Pacing: Terse call-and-response; the pause before the cloth revelation is the emotional center of the segment
Accent: Blaze — tight authority; Henchman — measured, professional

### SAMPLE CONTEXT
Blaze has found nothing in his building. Elsewhere, one of his men has found three things: a datachip fragment, security camera footage, and a cloth soaked in blood. The DNA confirmation at the end of the call is a door swinging shut on any remaining doubt.

#### TRANSCRIPT
“Yes? Find anything?” He said, turning to leave the room; there was nothing here.

“I believe we did, a fragment of a datachip and the footage from a nearby camera.”

“Good, I’m heading your way, upload the footage and I’ll review it on the way.”

“There’s something else, sir,” The man said, hesitating before finishing the sentence, “A small bloodsoaked cloth.”

Blaze’s blood boiled—he almost saw red for a second before tempering his rage; It could be honed towards the Daemon later.

“Good find. Any DNA?” Blaze said through gritted teeth.

“Yes sir, it’s a match.”
