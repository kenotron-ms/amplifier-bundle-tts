## SCENE 6 — CHASE CAN’T SLEEP

S06-001 [NARRATOR] Chase lay in his bed next to Lia. Everything was too hot under the blankets but too cold out of them. Could he really leave the Scavs? What would he do to provide for his family? He didn’t like his job with them but it beat typing away at a computer like a corpo. This was stupid. He sat up, making sure not to wake Lia. Maybe he was thirsty? He got out of bed and put on his casualwear, slowly making his way to the kitchen. He opened a cabinet and grabbed a glass from it, filling it up with the faucet in the sink. He took a sip of it as he padded over to the window. He wouldn’t miss the view, that’s for sure. Large neon signs and floating advertisements filled the sky outside, casting a harsh yet comforting glow into the living room. He stood there, looking out the window in a half-asleep daze. His senses were dulled, which is why he nearly dropped the glass when someone started pounding on the front door.
